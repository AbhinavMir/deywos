Deywos is a suite of tools in the blockchain devtooling space.

Deywos, the name, in Proto-Indo-European mythology, represents the sky or daylight deity. This figure is the precursor to many sky and thunder gods in ancient Indo-European religions, embodying divine power, order, and light.
